About the Artist

Hello :) My name is Elyse Cheng and I am a 2nd year student at UCI. I sign my art with Freaks because it is the nickname my friends gave me xD. I am an undeclared Humanities Major currently, but planning to declare German studies as soon as the quarter is over. I like to draw in my free time and have the most fun drawing tactical gear and detailed outfits/uniforms. Some of my biggest inspirations are Maki Kusumoto, Yasushi Nirasawa, Harry Clarke, Ivan Bilibin, and Aubrey Beardsley.
